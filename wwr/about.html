Rafting began as a practical means of transportation, with early civilizations using rivers to move goods and people. Over time, it evolved into an adventurous activity, especially in the 19th century when explorers navigated wild rivers for discovery. By the mid-20th century, rafting became a recreational sport, attracting thrill-seekers worldwide. Today, it combines teamwork, safety, and adrenaline, making it a popular outdoor experience.

History

Rafting Ant is an imaginary outdoor adventure company founded in the early 2000s, expanding across four countries and multiple U.S. states. It offers guided rafting experiences, training programs, and eco-tourism initiatives. Known for its innovation and safety standards, the company connects people with nature while promoting environmental awareness. Its diverse locations provide unique river experiences for beginners and experts alike.
